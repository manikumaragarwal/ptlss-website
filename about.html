About Pt. Laxmi Shanker Saraswati Inter College

A legacy of excellence, a commitment to nurturing young minds

Our Story

Established in the year 2003, Pt. Laxmi Shanker Saraswati Inter College has been a beacon of knowledge in Kanpur for over 25 years, dedicated to shaping young minds into responsible citizens. Founded with a vision to provide quality education beyond textbooks, we balance academics, values, and modern skills.

Under the visionary leadership of our Director, Mr. Amber Tiwari, we strive to set high standards in school education. Our campus in Kalyanpur provides a safe, discipline-oriented environment where nurturing teachers guide every student towards a distinct and successful future.

From nursery to senior secondary, we focus on character development, creative expression, and physical wellness, ensuring our alumni are confident and compassionate individuals.

Our Vision

To make the school a leading educational institution in Kanpur, known for academic excellence, moral values, innovative teaching methods, and holistic student development.

Our Mission

  • Deliver high-quality education with modern teaching practices.
  • Nurture students intellectually, morally, and socially.
  • Create a safe, disciplined, and inspiring learning environment.
  • Empower teachers with continuous training and innovation.
  • Prepare students for national and global opportunities.
