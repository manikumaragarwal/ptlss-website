About Our Academics

At our school, excellence is not just a goal, it is a way of life. We are committed to nurturing students
who are not only academically strong but also responsible, confident, and well-rounded individuals.

Our students consistently achieve outstanding results, both in academics and in co-curricular activities. They actively participate and excel in inter-school and state-level competitions across a wide range of areas, including sports, debates, yoga, chess, art and craft, swimming, and karate. Whether it is securing top academic positions, winning medals, or expressing creativity through the arts, our learners strive for excellence in everything they do.

Guided by experienced educators and supported by modern facilities, we provide an environment where every student is encouraged to explore their potential. We place equal emphasis on discipline, leadership, creativity, and healthy competition, helping students grow into confident individuals ready to face future challenges.

At Pt. Laxmi Shanker Saraswati Inter College, we believe education goes beyond textbooks. We aim to inspire a love for learning, build strong values, and prepare our students to lead with confidence, integrity, and purpose.
