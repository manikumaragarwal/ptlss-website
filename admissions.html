Admission Process

Simple steps to secure your child's place at Pt. L.S.S. Inter College

1

Enquiry

Visit our school or contact us for admission information and prospectus.

2

Application

Fill out the admission form with required documents and photographs.

3

Interaction

Informal interaction with the child and parents with the school management.

4

Assessment

Age-appropriate assessment to understand the child's learning level.

5

Admission

Admission confirmed upon completion of formalities and fee payment.

Age Criteria

  • PG 3+ Years
  • Jr. KG 4+ Years
  • Sr. KG 5+ Years
  • Class 1 6+ Years

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • Transfer Certificate (Original) for Class 2 onwards
  • Passport size photographs of student (3)
  • Passport size photographs of parents (2 each)
  • Aadhar Card of student and parents
